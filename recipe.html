Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description: This is an easy sandwich to make!

  • Time: 4 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Peanut Butter A lot!
Jelly A lot!
Bread Two slices

Steps

  1. Place bread on plate
  2. Place peanut butter on bread
  3. Place jelly on other piece of bread
  4. Put the slices together
